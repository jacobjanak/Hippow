Proof-of-work is currently the most popular method of securing blockchains. However, most proof-of-work blockchains have one major flaw: they're not scalable. They're slow, they're inefficient, and they require a ton of energy, like a hippo.
That's why Hippow was created.
The Hippow blockchain is fast, lightweight, and efficient. Hippow doesn't need to waste any energy to keep the blockchain secure. Before we get to the detailed explanation, let's first discuss how you can get started earning Hippow coins.

Getting Started

By simply visiting this website, you've already created your own personal Hippow wallet. Your wallet is currently displaying your balance in the top right corner of the screen. Hover over your wallet to see your public address and your private key.
Like with any other blockchain, your public address is safe to give out. That is the address people use to send you Hippow coins. On the other hand, your private key must be kept private at all times. Anyone who has your private key can steal your coins!
Now that you have a wallet, it's time to fill it up. You can earn Hippow coins by helping to secure the Hippow network. This process is simple. Here's how to earn coins:
1. Locate the blockchain tab on the left side of the screen. This shows every transaction that has ever taken place on the Hippow network.
2. Click on a "Pending" transaction. Try refreshing the blockchain if there aren't any pending transactions.
3. Every transaction has an image associated with it. To approve the transaction, you must be the first person to guess the secret word for that image. You have unlimited guesses. Correct guesses earn Hippow coins!
4. Here are some guessing tips: The secret word will always be an object that you can see somewhere in the image, usually in the background. It will always be a noun, but never a proper noun. To familiarize yourself with this process, you can click on any "Confirmed" transaction to see what the secret word was for that transaction's image.
