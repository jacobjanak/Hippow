Welcome

Spots.cc is a new cryptocurrency on its own native blockchain.
To begin earning Spots, click on an unconfirmed transaction and try to guess the secret word associated with the given image. This confirms the transaction and is equivalent to Bitcoin mining.
Spot transactions use a unique consensus algorithm that is based on the standard proof-of-work method. Consensus algorithms are a necessary part of blockchain technology because they prevent cheating. Compared to other proof-of-work blockchains, Spots.cc is at least 99.99% more energy efficient because it utilizes the abilities of humans, rather than computers, to do work.

Besides being energy efficient, this unique blockchain offers several other benefits. For one, it is the only cryptocurrency you can "mine" directly from your browser. Other blockchains require you to download complicated mining software and many only work when run on a GPU. By comparison, Spots.cc makes it incredibly easy to get started earning crypto.
Another added benefit is that the usability of Spots.cc makes it a great educational tool. Never before has it been so easy to explore a blockchain and understand the process of mining cryptocurrencies with hands-on experience. My hope is that this project demystifies cryptocurrencies for new investors with its simple and transparent process.
